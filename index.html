Bio

Blending the musical backgrounds of international members, AYAKASHI brings a fresh and innovative form of Japanese music to the Bay Area through melodic compositions and energetic performances. Featuring soaring vocals from Saitama - Japan, a beautiful combination of classical piano, blazing guitar solos and groovy rhythms, AYAKASHI channels the essence of classic J-pop and J-rock in a musical interpretation that is sure to entertain.

Members

  • Stephane (guitar) - France
  • Chelsea (vocal) - Japan
  • Alonso (drums) - USA
  • Rei (bass) - Japan/UK
  • Yuin (keyboard) - Australia
